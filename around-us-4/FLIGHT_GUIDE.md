### Pitch (W/S) - Nose Up/Down
- **W** - Pitches nose DOWN (descend)
- **S** - Pitches nose UP (climb)

Think of it like leaning forward/backward in a car.

### Roll (A/D) - Banking
- **A** - Roll LEFT (left wing down)

This tilts the aircraft side to side.

### Yaw (Q/E) - Turning
- **Q** - Turn LEFT
- **E** - Turn RIGHT

Like turning a steering wheel.

### Throttle (Shift/Ctrl) - Speed
- **SHIFT** - Speed up
- **CTRL** - Slow down

Controls how fast you're flying.

## âš ï¸ Common Mistakes

### Stalling (Too Slow)
- **Problem:** Going below 20 knots
- **Fix:** Increase throttle immediately!

### Over-Pitching
- **Problem:** Pulling up too hard
- **Fix:** Small pitch changes work best

### Uncontrolled Roll
- **Problem:** Aircraft keeps rolling
- **Fix:** Tap opposite roll direction to level

### Too Fast on Descent
- **Problem:** Speeding up while going down
- **Fix:** Reduce throttle, pitch up slightly
